Agora: Project Showcase

Agora: Project Showcase is a repository of projects created by computer science students and faculty at Calvin College.

These projects are hosted as examples of what students can expect to create in class or for fun while taking classes at Calvin College.

Projects that are hosted on this page have been approved by the Computer Science department at Calvin College, but this does not mean that they are perfect.

Many of these projects were made in the introductory computer science classes, and as such should not be held to the same standards as other software.

If you have enjoyed looking at these projects or would like to know more, please visit the website for the Computer Science Department at Calvin College.

Agora was begun in Fall 2015 by two senior computer science students, Drew Campo and Corwin Webster.

The next fall, the project was revisited by two more senior computer science students, Joel Stehouwer and Andrew Lang.
